Rise and Shine Breakfast

The Rise & Shine Breakfast is an opportunity to educate the community about the services and programs Boys & Girls Clubs of Greater Houston provides to the more than 33,000 at-risk youth through after school and summer programs. There is no cost to attend the breakfast, however space is limited and attendees are asked to make a contribution to support the thousands of youth and teens who need us most in our community, we hope you will join us.

Great Futures Dinner

About the Great Futures Dinner Join us as we celebrate Kroger for their partnership with Boys & Girls Clubs of Greater Houston! The Great Futures Dinner is the premier annual fundraiser of Boys & Girls Clubs of Greater Houston. This even showcases how youth are positively impacted by our Clubs every day and how the Clubs have helped them overcome seemingly insurmountable obstacles. We are grateful for the support of our community and hope you will join us.

For more information on attending our events or if you have other concern, please contact: Sarah Burns, sburns@bgcgh.org
